About Us

Streams of Purpose is a registered community-based, non-profit organization dedicated to advancing health, social well-being, and sustainable development in underserved communities. Founded on the principles of equity, compassion, and dignity, our team is united by a shared commitment to making healthcare accessible and empowering people to lead healthier, more fulfilled lives.

Through innovative outreach programs, strategic partnerships, and the mobilization of passionate volunteers, we address both the immediate and long-term needs of marginalized populations. Our initiatives range from organizing medical camps and mobile clinics to providing essential supplies and educational resources. We believe that lasting change comes from holistic, purpose-driven action and invite all who share our values to join the movement.

Our Story

Every movement begins with an idea—a spark that ignites change. For Streams of Purpose, that spark was Andrew Otieno, who envisioned a world where healthcare, dignity, and opportunity flowed freely, without barriers.

Having worked in public health and seen firsthand the inequities in medical access, Andrew recognized that underserved communities didn’t just need aid; they needed long-term solutions, empowerment, and sustainable outreach. He knew this mission couldn’t be carried alone, so he shared the vision with two friends who shared his passion—Silvester Okoth and Karen Wangeci.

Together, they transformed an idea into action. What started as conversations about health disparities and community struggles soon became a fully realized initiative—Streams of Purpose.

Starting with small health camps, they organized mobile clinics, health education programs, and support initiatives, pulling in volunteers and building partnerships that could amplify their impact. Andrew, now serving as the National Officer for SCOPH, continues to guide the organization alongside Silvester and Karen, ensuring that the vision remains strong and the mission continues to grow.

What began as an idea shared among friends has now become a movement, proving that when purpose meets action, change is inevitable.

Organization Structure

Streams of Purpose operates with a clear and transparent organizational structure designed to ensure efficiency, accountability, and meaningful impact. Our leadership and volunteer teams work in harmony to plan, implement, and evaluate our programs.

  • Executive Director (Founder): Provides overall vision, leadership, and represents the organization at the national and international level.
  • Programs Lead (Health & Outreach): Develops and manages health and outreach initiatives, ensuring they meet the needs of our target communities.
  • Finance and Partnerships Lead: Oversees budgeting, financial management, donor relations, and cultivates partnerships for sustainability and growth.
  • Logistics & Operations Officer: Coordinates logistics for events, mobilizes resources, and ensures smooth internal operations.
  • Communications Officer: Manages public relations, social media, branding, and stakeholder engagement to promote the organization’s image and outreach.
  • Additional Officers: May include Monitoring & Evaluation, Gender Affairs, Volunteer Coordination, or Regional Outreach, appointed as needed.
  • Volunteers: Play a crucial role in implementing programs, engaging communities, and supporting all organizational activities. Their dedication and passion drive our mission forward.

The leadership team meets regularly to plan, review, and coordinate activities, ensuring every voice is heard and every effort counts.

Our Programs

Medical Outreach

Our medical outreach program brings vital healthcare services to remote and marginalized areas. We conduct regular medical camps, offer mobile clinics, and provide free health screenings for conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, and more. Our dedicated team of volunteers and health professionals ensure that those who need care most receive it with dignity and respect.

Health Education

We believe that knowledge is power. Through workshops, school visits, and community forums, we educate people on important health topics including hygiene, nutrition, maternal and child health, mental health, and prevention of communicable and non-communicable diseases. Our goal is to inspire informed choices for healthier lives.

Community Support

Streams of Purpose is committed to supporting the most vulnerable. We distribute food packages, hygiene kits, clothing, and educational materials to orphans, the elderly, people with disabilities, and street families. Our support programs are designed not only to provide relief but also to restore hope and dignity in every individual we reach.
